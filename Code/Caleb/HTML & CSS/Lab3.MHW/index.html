Sustainable Urban Logging

If you are having a tree removed because of storm damage or disease, please consider us. Our mission is to save urban hardwoods from going up in smoke, compost, or waste.

We are a (sometimes) free service to have the intact downed tree trunk removed from your site or yard, saving you the time and expense of cutting, chipping, and transporting or disposing the wood.

Learn MoreList of Desired Wood Species

We are not a tree removal, thinning or pruning service.

We only wish to partner with you and your arborist in finding a home for all the beautiful urban hardwoods that otherwise would have become firewood, chips or landfill.

Our Services

Moving Logs

Our boom crane truck is capable of lifting logs and weights up to 9000 lbs. We also have a 18' car hauler trailer we utilize regularly.

Milling Logs

We mill up to 24" diameter logs on our LT40 hydraulic Woodmizer bandsaw mill. For logs wider than 24" diameter, we use a portable Granberg chainsaw mill.

Kiln Drying Wood

We charge $0.75 per board foot when drying lumber or slabs. Our dehumidification kiln can handle slabs up to 16' in length.

Slabs

We have elm, maple, and walnut slabs available in a variety of lengths, thicknesses and widths.
